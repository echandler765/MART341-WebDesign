Website Idea One

Favorite Sports Team

I have an idea about a website where it's centered around your favorite sports team. Let's take the New York Yankees for example and once you select the Yankees as your favorite team, the webpage will then show every single aspect of that team imaginable. The web page will show everything from the scores of their previous games to their upcoming games. It will also show promotions for each game and links to help guide people that are not from the area who are planning to come to the games in order to make sure the user is not going down a rabbit hole to find things. I would make the tone something to reflect all things centered around the team so that being said, I would say the tone would be semi professional while being fun at the same time.
