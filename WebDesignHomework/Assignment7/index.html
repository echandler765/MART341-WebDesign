Horror Movie Must Watches




Fall is right around the corner and we all know what that means, Halloween and spooky season will be upon us and what better way is there to celebrate Halloween and spooky season than to watch some horror movies! Well sit back and let me show you some of the best horror movies to keep the Halloween spirit high flying!

The Classics:

  • The Shining: An all-time classic about a hotel owner who slowly becomes more insane by the day due to the supernatural phenomena occuring within his hotel. If you like psychologicl thriller's, this is an instant classic!

  • Texas Chainsaw Massacre: A group of friends become stranded in rural Texas when they run into a not so friendly family of cannibals. If you like gore and blood, this is always a great one!

  • The Conjuring: The Conjuring follows a pair of paranormal investigators who are brought to a house with a heavy demonic pressence that terrorizes the family living there. If you like the paranormal and the supernatural, The Conjuring will be sure to freak you out!

  • The Blair Witch Project: The Blair With Project follows three film students that journey into the Black Hills to document the lgenedary Blair Witch when things start to get pretty freaky. If you're into FF films, you'll love the birth of found footage movies in the Blair Witch Project.

Underrated:

  1. From: A TV show about people who get trapped in a town and can never leave. To make matters worse, monsters come out at nighht that take the form of humans but certainly are not. If you're looking for a show that will keep you on edge, this is the one

  2. The Descent: A group of friends go on a hike in the Appalachian mountains and come across a cave. The group gets stuck in the cave and tries to find their way out and soon find out that they are not alone. If you like jumpscares, this one is for you!

  3. The Invisible Man: This film follows a woman who is living alone in her house in the woods and a man tries to break into her home. The plot twist is that she is deaf and has no idea that she's in the same house as a criminal. If you like mass amounts of suspense, this movie is for you!

There is no better time to watch a good horror movie than in the month of October and I sure hope that I can provide you some great options to watch with your friends and family for a good scare. If any of you have any suggestions on movies I should watch and review, let me know and I will do my best to watch and give honest feedback. Happy watching this spooky season!
