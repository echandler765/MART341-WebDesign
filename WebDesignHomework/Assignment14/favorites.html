Favorites to Win the 2024-2025 Championship

Auburn Tigers

Auburn retains an All-American center and good contributors from last year. After a disappointing early exit in March, the Tigers are ready for revenge with a deep team poised to make a deep run in March.

Gonzaga Bulldogs

Gonzaga returns almost all of last year's team including two transfers. Strong continuity could bode well for a Gonzaga team that has loads of success yet they still are looking for their first champsionship.

Kansas Jayhawks

Kansas seemed to have a good team last year but it just was not meant to be with a 2nd round exit. The Jayhawks loaded up in the transfer portal and retained starters from last year and appear to be one of the favorites to win it all come March.
