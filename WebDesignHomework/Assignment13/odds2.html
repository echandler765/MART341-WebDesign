Understanding Sports Betting Odds

How Plus and Minus Odds Work

In sports betting, odds represent the probability of an event happening and determine the potential payout. Plus odds (+) show how much you could win with a $100 bet. Minus odds (-) indicate how much you need to bet to win $100.
